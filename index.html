FAQ

Questions Before You Go Solar?

Here are quick answers to common questions about solar installation, subsidy, and maintenance.

How much does a solar system cost?

Cost depends on system size, roof condition, panel quality, inverter type, and subsidy eligibility. Contact us for a site-based quote.

How much electricity can I save?

Savings depend on your usage, sanctioned load, system capacity, sunlight, and billing pattern.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations can be completed quickly after survey, approval, materials, and documentation are ready.

Is government subsidy available?

Government subsidy may be available for eligible residential customers under applicable schemes. We help with guidance and documentation.

What is Net Metering?

Net metering lets eligible solar customers export extra electricity to the grid and receive billing adjustment as per utility rules.

How long do solar panels last?

Quality solar panels are commonly designed for long-term use, often with performance warranties from manufacturers.

Do you provide maintenance?

Yes. We provide cleaning, inspection, repair, performance checks, and annual maintenance contracts.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Aravalli District and customers all across Gujarat, India.
